The Counting Systems by Shahar Marcus

Wait a sec, Counting Systems?

Yep! The extra Counting Systems are a way of representing numbers in a way that is easy and efficient for computers to understand.

There are multiple widely used counting systems, that you should know about:

Decimal The counting system we're all used too...
You wont really learn anything new about this system, but You will learn how counting systems works.
Binary The counting system that you app is getting compiled to - The 1s and 0s. Youll also learn cool stuff you can do with binary numbers, inside your code!
Externs The Language Of Colors...
Learn about the haxe-decimal format, its major use case, and why its used like that.
